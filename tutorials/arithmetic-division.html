What is Division?

Division is splitting a number into equal parts. For example, 12 ÷ 3 means splitting 12 into 3 equal groups: each group has 4.

How Division Works

  • 8 ÷ 2 = 4
  • 15 ÷ 3 = 5
  • 9 ÷ 3 = 3

Practice Problems

  2. 18 ÷ 2 = ?
  3. 20 ÷ 5 = ?

Complex Example

Dividing Large Numbers: What is 144 ÷ 12?

Step 1: Set up the division: 144 ÷ 12

Step 2: 12 goes into 14 once (1 × 12 = 12), remainder 2. Bring down the 4 to make 24.

Step 3: 12 goes into 24 two times (2 × 12 = 24), remainder 0.

Final Answer: 144 ÷ 12 = 12

This example shows how to use long division to solve division problems with larger numbers.
