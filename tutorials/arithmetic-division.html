What is Division?

Division is splitting a number into equal parts. For example, 12 ÷ 3 means splitting 12 into 3 equal groups: each group has 4.

How Division Works

  • 8 ÷ 2 = 4
  • 15 ÷ 3 = 5
  • 9 ÷ 3 = 3
