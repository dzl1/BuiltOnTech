What is Cosine?

Cosine is a trigonometric function. In a right triangle, cosine of an angle is the ratio of the length of the adjacent side to the hypotenuse.

How to Use Cosine

  • cos(θ) = adjacent / hypotenuse
  • Example: If the adjacent side is 4 and the hypotenuse is 5, cos(θ) = 4/5 = 0.8
