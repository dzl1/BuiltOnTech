What is Multiplication?

Multiplication is repeated addition. For example, 3 × 4 means adding 3 four times: 3 + 3 + 3 + 3 = 12.

How Multiplication Works

  • 2 × 3 = 6
  • 4 × 5 = 20
  • 7 × 2 = 14

Complex Example

Multiplying Large Numbers: What is 23 × 17?

Step 1: Break 17 into 10 and 7. So, 23 × 17 = 23 × (10 + 7)

Step 2: Multiply 23 × 10 = 230

Step 3: Multiply 23 × 7 = 161

Step 4: Add the results: 230 + 161 = 391

This method (distributive property) helps you multiply large numbers by breaking them into smaller, easier parts.
