What is Tangent?

Tangent is a trigonometric function. In a right triangle, tangent of an angle is the ratio of the opposite side to the adjacent side.

How to Use Tangent

  • tan(θ) = opposite / adjacent
  • Example: If the opposite side is 3 and the adjacent side is 4, tan(θ) = 3/4 = 0.75
