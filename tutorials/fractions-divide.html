How to Divide Fractions

To divide fractions, flip the second fraction (find its reciprocal) and multiply.

  • 1/2 ÷ 1/4 = 1/2 × 4/1 = 4/2 = 2
  • 2/3 ÷ 1/6 = 2/3 × 6/1 = 12/3 = 4
