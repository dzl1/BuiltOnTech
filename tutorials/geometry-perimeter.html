What is Perimeter?

Perimeter is the distance around a shape. To find the perimeter, add up the lengths of all the sides.

How to Find Perimeter

  • Square: Perimeter = 4 × side
  • Rectangle: Perimeter = 2 × (length + width)

Practice Problems

  1. Find the perimeter of a square with side 5 cm.
  2. Find the perimeter of a rectangle with length 6 cm and width 2 cm.
