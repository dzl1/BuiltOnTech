What is Area?

Area is the amount of space inside a shape. It is measured in square units (like cm² or m²).

How to Find Area

  • Square: Area = side × side
  • Rectangle: Area = length × width
  • Triangle: Area = (base × height) / 2

Practice Problems

  1. Find the area of a square with side 4 cm.
  2. Find the area of a rectangle with length 5 cm and width 3 cm.
  3. Find the area of a triangle with base 6 cm and height 2 cm.
