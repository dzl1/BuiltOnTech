What is Subtraction?

Subtraction means taking away one number from another. It is the opposite of addition.

How Subtraction Works

For example, if you have 5 apples and give away 2, you have 5 - 2 = 3 apples left.

  • 7 - 4 = 3
  • 10 - 6 = 4
  • 8 - 2 = 6

Complex Example

Subtracting Across Zeros: What is 2000 - 587?

Step 1: Line up the numbers and subtract each column, borrowing as needed.

2000
- 587

Borrow from the thousands and hundreds places as needed:

2000 - 587 = 1413

Explanation: When subtracting across zeros, always borrow from the next non-zero digit to the left.
