What is Addition?

Addition is one of the four basic operations in arithmetic. It means putting two or more numbers together to find their total or sum.

How Addition Works

When you add two numbers, you are combining their values. For example, if you have 2 apples and get 3 more apples, you now have 2 + 3 = 5 apples.

  • 2 + 3 = 5
  • 7 + 1 = 8
  • 10 + 4 = 14

Tips for Addition

  • Start with the bigger number and count up.
  • Use your fingers or objects to help you count.
  • Practice with real-life examples, like adding toys or snacks.

Complex Example

Adding Multiple Numbers: What is the sum of 17 + 23 + 45 + 8?

Step 1: Add 17 and 23: 17 + 23 = 40

Step 2: Add 40 and 45: 40 + 45 = 85

Step 3: Add 85 and 8: 85 + 8 = 93

Final Answer: 17 + 23 + 45 + 8 = 93

This shows how you can break down a problem with many numbers into smaller steps, making it easier to solve.
