What is an Expression?

An algebraic expression is a combination of numbers, variables, and operations (like +, −, ×, ÷).

Examples of Expressions

  • 2x + 3
  • 4y - 7
  • 5a + 2b - 3

Practice Problems

  1. Simplify: 2x + 3x
  2. Evaluate: 4y - 7 for y = 2
  3. Simplify: 5a + 2b - 3 when a = 1, b = 2
