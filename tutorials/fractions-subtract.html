How to Subtract Fractions

To subtract fractions with the same denominator, subtract the numerators and keep the denominator. For different denominators, find a common denominator first.

  • 3/4 - 1/4 = 2/4 = 1/2
  • 5/6 - 1/6 = 4/6 = 2/3
